Chess Borodino

"Borodino" - the battle of Borodino in 1812. The main figures reflect the portrait similarity of the main participants in the Russian battle and the French armies. Russian army: Alexander I - Emperor of Russia (1777-1825); Kutuzov (1745-1813) - Field Marshal, Commander-in-Chief of the Russian Army; Barclay de Tolly (1761-1818) - Field Marshal, Earl, Minister of War, Commander of the 1st Western Army; Bagration (1765-1812) - Infantry General, Prince, commander of the 2nd Western Army. French Army: Napoleon I (1769-1821) - Emperor of France; Joachim Murat (1767-1815) - Marshal of France, King of Naples, commander of the cavalry; Louis Nicolas Davout (1770-1823) - Marshal of France, Duke of Auerstedt, Commander 1st building; Eugene Bogarne (1781-1824) - Viceroy of the Kingdom of Italy, commander 4th building, stepson of Napoleon.

  1. Material: chess - porcelain, glaze, gilding, silvering, salts, chandeliers, paints.
  2. Dimensions: king - 130 mm, pawn - 100 mm
  3. Chess Board Case Size:560 х 280 х 140
  4. Fields are drawn from the veneer of various valuable species of wood - mahogany, Pacific nut, Karelian birch, maple, beech, birch and other species.
  5. Inside the figure, each lies in its own cell.
  6. Board manufacturing Krichko V.A.
