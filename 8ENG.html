Chess Italian Swiss Campaign 1799

Italian - Swiss campaign of 1799 of the Great Russian commander A.V.Suvorov and his famous passage through the Alps. The work depicts the famous commanders of the Russian and French army. Their portrait resemblance is reflected. A.V.Suvorov - Generalissimo of all Russian troops, Field Marshal-Commander-in-Chief of the Allied Forces. Major General A.G. Rosenberg; Major General P.I. Bagration. French army: A. Massena - Division General - Commander of the French Helvetic Army. J.S.E. MacDonald - Division General - Commander of the French Neapolitan Army. J.V. Morot - division general - commander of the French Italian army ..

  1. Material: chess - porcelain, glaze, gilding, silvering, salts, chandeliers, paints.
  2. Dimensions: king - 200 mm, pawn - 110 mm
  3. Chess Board Case Size: 680 х 340 х 200
  4. Fields are drawn from the veneer of various valuable species of wood - mahogany, Pacific nut, Karelian birch, maple, beech, birch and other species.
  5. Inside the figure, each lies in its own cell.
  6. Board manufacturing Krichko V.A.
