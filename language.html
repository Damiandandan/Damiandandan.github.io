Handmade Gift Chess

I have been seriously engaged in chess since 1991. As a result, I now have 12 different chess styles and themes. Each of them is a separate segment of my life and my aspirations. Almost half of my collection is dedicated to the glorious events of the Russian State. I tried to echo these great events as accurately as possible reflected in chess. Historically true costumes, details of that glorious era and portrait similarity of the main figures of that time are an integral part of every chess game of the Glorious Russian Victory series. Reflecting this topic, I primarily express gratitude and respect to our Great Ancestors and their glorious accomplishments. The development of the workshop leads to the formation of creative alliances with famous artists. As a result, new unexpected and interesting solutions. Now I have several new projects in development. The creative process continues.
